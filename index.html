Meet Uninova

Uninova Nigeria is a food industry service company with core vision of helping companies to adhere to Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) and meet regulatory requirements – NAFDAC and SON. We are registered by Environmental Health Officer's Registration Council of Nigeria (EHORECON) as service provider in pest control through our subsidiary God’s Chemical and Pest Control Services Limited, where we undertake elimination of pest of public health importance in industries, banks, hospitals, hotels, courts, libraries and many more.
