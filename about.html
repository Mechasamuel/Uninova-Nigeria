About Uninova Nigeria

Get to know about Our Company

Uninova Nigeria is a food industry service company with core vision of helping companies adhere to Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) and meet regulatory requirements from NAFDAC and SON. We are registered by Environmental Health Officer's Registration Council of Nigeria (EHORECON) as service provider in pest control through our subsidiary God’s Chemical and Pest Control Services Limited, where we undertake elimination of pest in industries, banks, hospitals, hotels, courts, libraries and many more. We also undertake registration of trade mark and logo with ministry of trade and investment, incorporation of companies, NGOs and business name with the NIgerian Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC).
We are certified food scientists so we undertake services in: plant design & installation of food processing equipments, water treatment, and many more.
